Global Standards

Faceoff is a fully licensed automotive import entity operating under Nigerian law with all documentation in order. We adhere to NAFDAC, SON, and Nigerian Customs compliance frameworks. Every transaction is transparent — you receive a full breakdown of vehicle cost, shipping, duty, and our service fee before commitment.

We work with internationally certified inspection partners in each source country to guarantee the condition and history of every vehicle before it leaves the ground.

Regional Reach

Headquartered in Lagos with operational presence in Abuja, Port Harcourt, and Enugu, our logistics network extends across Nigeria and into West Africa. We deliver directly to your city — or arrange a supervised handover at our Lagos facility.

Cross-border clients in Ghana, Togo, and Benin Republic are served through our bonded partner network, with full customs documentation handled end-to-end.
